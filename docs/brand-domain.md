# Brand and Domain Recommendation

## Recommendation

Do not use `MetaMarkets.cc` for this product.

- "Markets" suggests finance, trading, or a broad marketplace rather than building-direct luxury rentals.
- "MetaMarkets" already has strong historical association with an ad-tech analytics company.
- `.cc` is less familiar and trustworthy for a US renter-facing real-estate product than `.com` or a deliberate `.co`.

Keep **PropertyVault** as the working product name while conducting a trademark/domain clearance pass, or use a clearer descriptive launch domain.

## Candidate launch domains

RDAP returned no registration record on June 14, 2026 for:

- `BuildingDirectNYC.com` - strongest descriptive option for the launch market
- `DirectRentalNYC.com` - clear but less building-first
- `RentTheBuilding.com` - memorable editorial option
- `BuildingDirect.co` - scalable beyond NYC, but `.co` has typo risk

Availability can change immediately and an RDAP 404 is not a purchase guarantee. Confirm at the chosen registrar and run trademark clearance before committing.
