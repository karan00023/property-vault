# Data and media policy

- Never infer or fabricate rents, availability, unit counts, floor plans, or photography.
- Store exact source URLs and fetch timestamps for every public field.
- Treat AI output as a low-confidence research lead until reviewed.
- Publish photography only with explicit license/source metadata.
- Floor plans must be official PDFs/images or verified SightMap/Engrain embeds.
- Unknown data is a first-class state and should be shown honestly.
